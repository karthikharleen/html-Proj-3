Fun Fact about us!

Did you know that at Parotta Paradise, we make our parottas using a unique technique called "kallu shapu," which involves stretching and twirling the dough in the air to create the signature flaky layers?
This method requires great skill and precision, and our chefs have been trained to perfect it over many years of practice.
So, not only do our parottas taste amazing, but they're also a sight to behold as they're being made in our kitchen!
